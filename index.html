The King - Virat Kohli

The best batsman in the world

Virat kolhi

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He currently represents Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL and Delhi in domestic cricket. Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket, and the best of 21st century. His consistency, technique, and ability to score runs in all formats of the game make him a cricketing icon.


The Greatest Batsman
